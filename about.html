ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Why Should You Choose Us?

Innovate General Insurance (IGI) Limited, established in February 2015 in Lusaka, Zambia, is a forward-thinking non-life insurance company specializing in short-term risk coverage across construction, motor, marine, engineering, and commercial lines. As part of Innovate Holdings, which includes Innovate Capital Solutions and Venture Capital Partners, IGI is dedicated to providing innovative and customer-centric insurance solutions. The company emphasizes convenience, value, and a superior customer experience, integrating comprehensive risk management with unique value-added services to meet diverse client needs. IGI's mission is to lead the market with innovative products and quality service, leveraging technology and strategic partnerships to enhance accessibility and affordability, while promoting financial literacy among underserved and SME sectors.

Our Strategic Focus

IGI's value proposition centers on convenience and value through:

1. ..Exceptional Customer Experience:Offering efficient after-sales risk cover renewal processes supported by effective communication, fostering a superior service culture.
2. ..Customer Segmentation: Understanding the unique needs of different customer segments to manage risks and enhance customer retention.
3. ..Integrated Solutions: Delivering value-added services beyond standard offerings. For example, corporate clients benefit from additional services such as obtaining road tax and fitness documentation along with motor insurance.

Overview:

Innovate General Insurance (IGI) Limited is a non-life insurance company based in Lusaka, Zambia, founded in February 2015. It is part of **Innovate Holdings**, which also includes **Innovate Capital Solutions Limited** (SME lending) and **Venture Capital Partners Limited** (venture capital). IGI offers a range of short-term insurance solutions, including coverage for construction, motor, marine, engineering, and commercial risks. The company emphasizes customer convenience and value by delivering a seamless after-sales experience, personalized risk management, and added services like assisting with road tax and fitness documentation for corporate clients. With a mission to provide innovative, customer-centric insurance products, IGI strives to become the preferred insurer in its markets by offering efficient, low-cost services and collaborating with partners to improve financial literacy and accessibility.

Mission:

To provide innovative, customer-centric insurance products that create value for our stakeholders. This mission is supported by delivering high-quality customer care, motivating our staff, and ensuring a seamless and efficient service experience.

Vision:

To be the preferred provider of insurance services in our chosen markets, recognized for our commitment to innovation, superior customer experience, and comprehensive risk management solutions.

Our Goals:

IGI aims to enhance customer satisfaction by delivering exceptional service and effective risk management, while expanding market reach through innovative products and strategic partnerships. The company focuses on promoting financial inclusion by offering accessible and affordable insurance solutions to underserved and SME sectors, alongside improving financial literacy. Operational excellence is a key goal, achieved by streamlining processes to reduce costs and enhance service delivery. IGI is dedicated to driving innovation to stay ahead of industry trends, fostering employee development to maintain a motivated workforce, and building robust risk management practices to minimize client exposure. Strong stakeholder relationships and a commitment to ethical business practices underpin all of IGI’s endeavors, ensuring integrity and mutual success.
