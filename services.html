Our Services

Innovate General Insurance (IGI) Limited offers a comprehensive range of insurance services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our services are designed to provide effective risk management, seamless policy administration, and innovative insurance solutions.

Product Development

We develop customized insurance products that address specific market needs, ensuring our clients receive innovative and relevant coverage options.

Policy Administration and Underwriting

Our policy administration and underwriting services ensure that insurance policies are managed efficiently, with accurate risk assessment and coverage alignment.

Claims Management

We offer a streamlined claims management process, helping our clients handle claims quickly and effectively to minimize disruption and ensure timely settlements.

Fronting

IGI provides fronting services, allowing companies to access insurance solutions through our established network while maintaining their own risk management strategies.

Corporate Risk Assessment & Formulation of Risk Portfolio

We conduct thorough risk assessments for corporate clients, formulating tailored risk portfolios to manage exposures effectively and safeguard business operations.

Insurance Training

Our training programs are designed to enhance insurance knowledge among clients and partners, promoting informed decision-making and effective risk management practices.

Risk Management

IGI offers comprehensive risk management services, providing expert advice and solutions to mitigate potential risks and protect assets.

For more information or to inquire about our services, please contact us.
